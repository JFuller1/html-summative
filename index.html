Favorite Movies


The purpose of this website is to share my love of movies with you, the user. You can see the list by clicking here . I have ranked these movies on five pieces of criteria which I think are extremely important for most movies to have.

Originality
- Who wants to see the same movie over and over?

Acting Quality
- An actors performance can make or break a movie, no matter how good the script is.

Script Quality
- A movie is nothing without an engaging story.

Pacing
- It is important for a movies pacing to be proper to avoid it being completly dull.

Characters
- I find that it is necessary to have characters with depth, interesting personalities and, most importantly, making the characters likeable
